💡 跨越封鎖：炫辰 前輩的雙語夾擊法則

為什麼在試算表用中文直接問 Gemini 會出現「不支援此語言」？前輩告訴你神級破解法：

直接下中文指令？

Google 試算表內建的 `=GEMINI()` 函式對中文指令審查極其嚴格，常常拋出 "The language is not supported" 的錯誤訊息，宣告失敗。

雙語夾擊 (Bilingual) 法則

核心秘訣：**使用英文給定 AI 角色與任務結構，並利用 `&` 串接您的中文表格資料。最後強烈指令要求以繁體中文 (Traditional Chinese) 回覆！**

流暢的橫向流水線

利用 Google Sheets 的資料參照，後一格的指令可以引用前一格的生成結果，像接力棒一樣讓 AI 幫你層層遞進、深度加工，一格產出一片天。

You MUST reply in Traditional Chinese (繁體中文) in a structured manner.")

=GEMINI("Based on the meeting minutes and the extracted action items, identify the 'Owner' (Person in charge) for each task. [Meeting Minutes]: " & B2 & " [Action Items]: " & C2 & " [Output Requirements]: 1. Match the task numbers from the Action Items (e.g., 1. Name, 2. Name). 2. If no specific person is mentioned, write '待確認' (To be confirmed). 3. You MUST reply in Traditional Chinese (繁體中文) and list names only.") 
=GEMINI("Based on the meeting minutes and the extracted action items, identify the 'Owner' (Person in charge) for each task.

[Meeting Minutes]: " & B2 & "
[Action Items]: " & C2 & "

[Output Requirements]:
1. Match the task numbers from the Action Items (e.g., 1. Name, 2. Name).
2. If no specific person is mentioned, write '待確認' (To be confirmed).
3. You MUST reply in Traditional Chinese (繁體中文) and list names only.")

[Function]: " & D2 & " [Constraints/Remarks]: " & C2 & " [Output Requirements]: 1. Do not include summary, intro or outro text. 2. You MUST reply in Traditional Chinese (繁體中文).")

[Supplementary Knowledge Base]: " & B2 & " [User Task]: " & C2 & " [Output Requirements]: 1. Generate catchy social media copy. Include 3 relevant hashtags. 2.
